Life-Developer PWP - Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, and Goal:

Purpose: This project is designed to focus on showing my personal journey through the beginning of a tech career, and the struggles of dealing with an autoimmune condition.

Audience: This project is for individuals trying to navigate through a career while also going through the struggles of a health condition.

Goal: The goal of this website is to demonstrate my efforts put into getting a career going while also sharing my personal struggles. This will hopefully allow others in a similar situation to feel a sense of motivation and support.

User Story:

As a user, I would want to be able to feel understood and recognized when I visit a support/testimonial website.

Persona:

Name: Julie S. Tillerman

Age: 25

Occupation: Graphic Designer

Profession: Julie is a Senior Graphic Designer for a start-up tech company. She takes pride in the precision, flow, and layout of her work. She was recently diagnosed with an autoimmune condition, and often becomes disheartened about her career as she navigates the real struggles of her condition.

Technology: Julie utilizes a lower-end MacBook Pro along with a logitech mouse, a decent sized Wacom tablet, and an OLED external monitor. This is often all connected using a laptop docking station that Julie does not know the name of. They use their computer for business and personal activities. They use a standard iPhone 14 for everyday use.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Having been recently diagnosed with a chronic health condition, Julie often times struggles with trying to get through the day without her condition interfering. She loves design and inspirational messages, especially if it is something she can directly identify with. She often times loses motivation because she feels like she's struggling with managing a career and a condition all on her own.

Frustrations and Needs: Julie is feeling stressed out from work, and her friends do not fully understand the things she struggles with. This struggle causes her to feel unsupported, unmotivated, and often times lost in feeling like nobody understands her. Her immediate support group only can do so much to show her support.

Goals: Julie is a curious individual who stumbles upon life-stories. She likes to find online content by word-of-mouth, and she wants to find someone she can identify with. Having someone voice their struggles with career/health in a very identifiable way, with quality resources, will help me to feel supported and inspired to move forward in life.
